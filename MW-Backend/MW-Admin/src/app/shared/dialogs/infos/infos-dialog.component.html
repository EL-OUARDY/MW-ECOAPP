Advanced Search :

Search normaly uses product's name, you could also type an expression in the search box that can extand searching to other properties like price, quantity, .. to acheive this all you have to do is prefixing your searching query with "$" or "#" signs and follow it with your expression
An expression could be like what you see in the exemples below.

#574995214 Will look for a product with this Id
$price = value Price equal a given value
$quantiy > 7 and $price = 9.99 Quantity greater then 7 and price equal 9.99
$price > 10 and price < 15 Price between 10 and 15
$color = 'pink' or color = 'green' Color eithe pink or green

NB: be aware that areas with characters value not numbers (such as: Name, Color, ..) should be wrapped within single qouts 'string' otherwise just pass the value directly
